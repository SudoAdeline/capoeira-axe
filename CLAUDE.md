## About this project

This is an AI-powered sound engineering tool that provides real-time feedback to musicians and producers. It's built by a musician, for musicians — someone who knows what good sound feels like, not just what it measures as.

### Core principles
- **Musical intuition first, technical specs second.** This tool should feel like a skilled engineer sitting next to you, not a spreadsheet.
- **Accessible language.** Not everyone knows what a compressor ratio means. Translate.
- **Honest feedback.** Don't flatter. Don't crush. Be the engineer who makes you better.

### Tech notes
- Keep the codebase clean. Future you will thank present you.
- Commit often. Small progress is still progress.
- When you're stuck, step away and sing something. Come back with fresh ears.
