# AXÉ — Capoeira Progression Guide

The ultimate web app for capoeiristas. Track your training, build your cord system, discover events, and connect with the global capoeira community.

## Features

### Training Plans — Regional & Angola

Toggle between two complete training systems:

**Regional / Contemporânea:**
- **Golpes** — Kicks & strikes from bênção to parafuso
- **Floreios** — Acrobatics & flow: aú, macaco, mortal and beyond
- **Jogo de Chão** — Ground game, esquivas, sweeps & malícia
- **Música & Cultura** — Berimbau, pandeiro, songs & history

**Angola:**
- **Ginga de Angola** — Low movement, chamadas, rasteiras, mandinga & ancestral philosophy
- **Música de Angola** — Toques, three berimbaus, ladainhas, chulas & the Angola sound

Each path has multiple levels with detailed skill breakdowns including key teaching cues, milestones, and timelines.

### Progress Tracking

Mark movements as mastered and track completion across all paths and levels with visual progress bars.

### Custom Training

Add your own training goals — describe what you want to work on and the app auto-categorizes it into the right path with its own progress tracking.

### Custom Cord System

Build your group's cord progression from scratch:
- Choose from 10 colors
- 4 pattern types: Solid, Half & Half, Striped, Gradient
- Add names and meanings
- Set your current cord level
- Reorder and manage your full cord system

### Favorite Songs

Save your favorite capoeira songs (corridos, ladainhas, chulas, quadras) with lyrics in the Música path.

### Events & Community

A full event system for the capoeira community:
- **Browse events** — rodas, workshops, batizados, festivals
- **Search & filter** — by name, location, group, type
- **Scope filtering** — Local, Regional, National, Global
- **Built-in calendar** — monthly view with event dots, day selection
- **Event details** — full info, organizer, price, registration links
- **Attendance** — confirm you're going, see who else is attending
- **Create events** — share your rodas and workshops with the community

### Design

- Dark warm Brazilian aesthetic with kinetic animated background
- Responsive design — works on mobile and desktop
- Bebas Neue + DM Sans typography
- Path-specific accent colors
- Smooth fade transitions and hover animations
